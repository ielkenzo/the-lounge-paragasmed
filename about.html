The Lounge by ParagasMed is the aesthetic arm of ParagasMed — a dermatology and cosmetic surgery practice founded by Dr. Michael Paragas to bring proper diagnosis and modern technology together under one roof.

Meet the physician

Dr. Michael Paragas

Dr. Michael C. Paragas is a Philippine-licensed physician practicing clinical and aesthetic dermatology and cosmetic surgery. He earned his medical degree from Our Lady of Fatima University in 2018 and passed the Physician Licensure Examination in 2019, going on to complete fellowship training in cosmetic surgery and clinical & aesthetic dermatology.

He founded ParagasMed in Calasiao, Pangasinan, and later opened The Lounge Clinic locations in Ortigas and at Civic Place, Alabang — extending the same physician-led approach to patients in Metro Manila.

Our purpose

What ParagasMed is built for

ParagasMed exists on the idea that cosmetic goals and medical skin health shouldn't be treated as separate problems — every plan starts with an accurate diagnosis.

✧

Diagnosis before treatment

Acne, hair loss, rashes and lesions are medical concerns first. Dr. Paragas examines and diagnoses before any cosmetic or energy-based procedure is recommended.

⌁

Evidence-based aesthetics

Injectables, skin boosters and laser or energy-based devices are chosen to fit the diagnosis and skin type — not simply the latest trend.

◇

A calmer clinic experience

The Lounge takes that same medical foundation and delivers it in a private, unhurried, boutique-style setting built around the patient.
